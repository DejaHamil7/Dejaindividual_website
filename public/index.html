Purpose

Many individuals are unable to attend college after high school, whether for personal or financial reasons.

This is a platform that assists individials to find employment, build skills, or obtain the necessary means connect them to employment or education facilities that rely less on fiancial means. This website will provide the resources to assist individuals toward a positive future without the use of college. this website additionally provides financial help resources through scholarships for those who wish to attend an institution too!
